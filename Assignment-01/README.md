## Exercise Sheet 1 – Bound States and Root-Finding Methods

Plots even and odd transcendental functions arising from the quantum finite square well to identify bound state solutions. Implements and compares three root-finding algorithms — bisection, Newton, and secant methods — applied to locate energy eigenvalues. Benchmarks all three methods across multiple tolerances, comparing convergence speed, iteration count, and residuals.

**Libraries:** NumPy, Matplotlib, Pandas, SciPy, time
